On Mondays, we take time to listen to our audience's opinions on a broad set of issues worth debating over by means of polls. Alternatively, we put to test their skills through engrossing quizzes. We disclose the results the following Sunday!
Tuesdays are reserved for the trusty Word of the Week segment, and its many extensions which serve as means to intensify your expertise in the English language.
While there is no single recipe to follow in order to become an excellent writer, we dedicate Wednesdays to hand out the best pointers we've compiled to help you improve your literary skills!
For the past two years, our much-loved segment, Humans of CBIT, brought CBIT's unsung heroes to light. This year, with its new extension, Humans of CCD, we spotlight the efforts of the quondam members of Communicando to remind us of their legacy and remind them of our love!
Every Thursday, we dive into the richness of the Hindi language and learn new words with साप्ताहिक शब्द along with phrases to enhance our vocabulary.
Ever felt blank when you opened Netflix, entered a book store, or frolicked in your kitchen to cook? Communicando presents a new segment called, "Weekends With CCD" to enlighten you with some rich and intriguing finds of ours - mentally stimulating books, entrancing films, and easy as pie recipes. Tune in every Friday!
We also feature book, movie and song reviews written by both our team and audience on Fridays to give you new insights into works of art, to introduce you to new books and movies, or to simply enjoy reading!
On Saturdays, we take a detour to visit our roots in Telugu and pick up new words with ‘ఈ వారపు పదం’ along with phrases to strengthen our grasp over our regional language.
On Sundays, we highlight the efforts of each of our team members from the senior team with 'CCD Behind The Scenes' as a token of our appreciation, because we believe that a club is only as strong as its team members.
